Why your kid needs this

Give them a head start on the world they're growing up into.

AI will be woven into nearly everything today's kids grow up to do. They're going to use it either way — the head start is understanding it: knowing how it works, what it's great at, and where it still needs a human.

🔍

Understand it, don't fear it

Kids learn what AI actually is — pattern-finding, prediction, training — by playing with it directly. Demystified early, it becomes a tool they command, not magic that commands them.

🧭

Judgment over memorization

When AI can answer any question, the valuable skill is asking great questions, spotting wrong answers, and knowing when to trust the machine. That's exactly what our missions train.

🚀

Builders, not just users

Every camper finishes with a Big Mission they built themselves with real AI behind it — and the confidence that comes from "I made this," not "I watched this."

"Code handles the loop. AI handles the judgment inside the loop. Humans handle the exceptions."
The mental model we teach
The killer platform

A learning world kids actually want to log into.

Camp doesn't end when the library session does. Every camper gets a login to our platform — built from scratch for ages 8–11, paced like a great game.

🎮

12 game-like missions

Full-screen, one idea at a time — built for busy brains. Kids sort, train, draw, and build their way through every concept. No walls of text, ever.

🎨

Real AI that really sees

Campers draw on a canvas and a real AI guesses their picture. They remix their homepage with AI-generated art. They finish by building with a live AI — the same kind professionals use.

🏅

Badges & character gear

Every mission earns custom badge art plus new gear for their animated character — capes, jetpacks, crowns. Progress kids can see and show off.

📅

Paced like a real camp

Missions unlock in waves across the week, so kids can't binge it all in one sitting — and always have a reason to come back. Parents love this part.

💬

A safe clubhouse

Campers chat with their cohort, see who's online, and cheer each other on — behind a profanity filter, with staff able to see every message. No email or personal info required.

🎧

Reads aloud & lo-fi beats

One tap and every mission reads itself aloud. Another tap starts lo-fi study music kids can remix live — a little taste of generative AI in itself. Accessible for every kind of learner.

In person · Louisville

🏛️ Hands-on labs at the public library

The platform is half the story. Campers meet in small groups at the public library in Louisville for guided, hands-on sessions — together, out loud, with mentors in the room.

  • Small groups with real mentors, not lecture rows
  • Build days: campers pair up and make things together
  • Demo day finale — families invited to see what kids built
  • A free public space families already know and trust
Designed responsibly

🧠 A child psychologist on the team

A practicing child psychologist helps design our curriculum and reviews every module — so the way we teach AI fits how kids ages 8–11 actually think, focus, and build confidence.

  • Short, satisfying missions matched to real attention spans
  • Feedback designed to build confidence, never shame mistakes
  • Healthy-use pacing: sessions end, kids go play
  • Honest, calm answers to the big questions — at kid level
