## Sourcing policy (read before adding images)

- **Default to public domain / CC0** (Wikimedia Commons, NASA, Openverse, Unsplash).
- **Historical figures/photos:** Wikimedia Commons, license verified per file.
- **Decorative/illustration:** prefer original generated art (Firefly/Canva) or the brand's
  Lucide icons + SVG — avoids licensing risk and stays on-brand.
- **Never** scrape Google Images or embed unlicensed/“all rights reserved” images in the
  product. Record every image's source + license in this file.
