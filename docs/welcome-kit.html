Welcome to the Founding Cohort

You're in. Here's how to get ready for July.

Your family is part of the very first Code the Future cohort — kids who will grow up understanding AI instead of just using it. Before sessions begin, here's everything you need to get set up and what the summer will look like.

Get set up in five minutes — do this together
1
Visit the platform

Go to codethefuture.net and tap Log In in the top right. Enter the family access password from your welcome email.

2
Build their character

Your camper creates their buddy — color, hair, face, gear. No email or real name needed; they pick a fun camp name.

3
Join the cohort

Enter the cohort code from your welcome email when the platform asks. That connects them to their group and their guides.

4
Start Mission 1

It takes about ten minutes and ends with their first arcade game and badge. They can replay any finished mission, any time.

What the summer looks like
One hour at the library, weekly

Small-group, hands-on sessions with mentors in the room. Your session day and time arrive by email before the first week.

Missions at home, paced

Twelve missions on the platform, unlocking in three waves across the week — designed so kids come back, not binge.

Badges, gear, and a Big Mission

Every mission earns badge art and character gear. The finale: building with a real AI, the same kind professionals use.

For parents — how we keep it safe
  • No personal data from kids. They join with a code — no email, no real names required.
  • Filtered, supervised chat. Every message passes a language filter and is visible to staff, including private ones.
  • A psychologist on the team. A specialist in child development helped design the platform and reviews every module.
  • Progress you can ask about. Staff dashboards track every camper and flag anyone who's stuck — just ask us anytime.
Before the first session
  • Do the five-minute setup above, together
  • Let them finish Mission 1 (and show you the badge)
  • A laptop or Chromebook with a browser — that's all the tech they need
  • Headphones help for the read-aloud and study music
Questions before July?

Reply to your welcome email anytime — you'll reach Mr. Jon directly. We'll send your library session schedule, cohort code, and family password before the first week. We can't wait to meet your builder.
